About Me

I completed my undergraduate and masters degrees at the University of Adelaide. My masters thesis “Quadrics & Quadrals, Combinatorics & Characterisations: a Survey of Subspaces”, supervised by Susan Barwick, collected known characterisations of quadrics as well as proving several new characterisations.
