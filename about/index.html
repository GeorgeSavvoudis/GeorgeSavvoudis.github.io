About Me

Hello! Welcome to my website. My name is George Savvoudis, I am a PhD candidate in Mathematics currently working at UP FAMNIT under the supervision of Michel Lavrauw. I completed my undergraduate and masters degrees at the University of Adelaide. My masters thesis “Quadrics & Quadrals, Combinatorics & Characterisations: a Survey of Subspaces”, supervised by Susan Barwick, collected known characterisations of quadrics as well as proving several new characterisations.
